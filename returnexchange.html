Returns & Exchange Policy

Our Policy

At HijabHeaven, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a straightforward returns and exchanges policy.

Returns

Items can be returned within 14 days of delivery for a refund or store credit. To be eligible for a return:

  • The item must be unused and in its original packaging.
  • A receipt or proof of purchase must be included.
  • Sale items are not eligible for returns.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item, contact our support team at support@hijabheaven.com.

Shipping

You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

For more help, please contact our support team. Thank you for shopping with HijabHeaven!
