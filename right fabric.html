CHHOSING RIGHT FABRIC FOR HIJABS & ABAYAS

When selecting fabric for your abaya and hijab, consider climate, comfort, occasion, and maintenance. For warm climates, opt for breathable fabrics like cotton, linen, or crepe. For cooler weather, heavier fabrics like velvet, satin, or heavier cotton blends can provide warmth and comfort. Fabrics like jersey, crepe, and cotton offer comfort and flexibility for daily wear.

Hijab Fabrics: Finding Your Ideal Match

1. Georgette

  • Features: Lightweight, slightly textured, and breathable.
  • Best For: Everyday wear and formal occasions.
  • Highlights: Hijab Heaven's Georgette collection includes Plain, Fluid, and Pre-sewn Instant options, offering versatility and ease of styling.

2. Modal

  • Features:Soft, breathable, and drapes beautifully.
  • Best For:All-day comfort and elegant looks.
  • Highlights:The Modal Hijabs come in various shades like Jet Black and Nude Ivory, providing both comfort and style.

3. Jersey

  • Features:Stretchy, opaque, and easy to wrap.
  • Best For:Active days and cooler climates.
  • Highlights:Hijab Heaven offers both standard and Ribbed Jersey options, ensuring a snug fit without pins.

4. Satin Silk

  • Features:Smooth, glossy finish with a luxurious feel.
  • Best For:Special events and formal gatherings.
  • Highlights:The Satin Silk collection, including the Opulence line, adds a touch of elegance to any outfit.

5. Viscose

  • Features:Lightweight, breathable, and soft.
  • Best For:Warm weather and casual wear.
  • Highlights:Options like Turkish Viscose and Crinkle Lawn provide comfort with a touch of texture.

Abaya Fabrics: Comfort Meets Elegance

1. Premium Quality Nidha

  • Features:Soft, lightweight, fully opaque with a subtle sheen.
  • Best For:All-season wear, including daily use and Umrah trips.
  • Highlights:The Premium Everyday Abaya + Hijab Set is crafted from Nidha fabric, ensuring comfort and modesty.

2. Imported Georgette

  • Features:Flowy, breathable, and elegant.
  • Best For:Formal events and special occasions.
  • Highlights:The Front Close Side Zip Printed Maxi Abaya showcases the beauty of Imported Georgette fabric.

Tips for Choosing the Right Fabric

  • Climate Consideration:Opt for breathable fabrics like Viscose or Georgette in warmer climates, and warmer materials like Jersey in cooler weather.
  • Occasion Suitability:Choose luxurious fabrics like Satin Silk for formal events, and comfortable options like Modal or Nidha for daily wear.
  • Maintenance Needs:Consider the care requirements; for instance, Nidha may require mild ironing, while Jersey is low-maintenance.
