FAQ'S

What is HijabHeaven?

HijabHeaven is your go-to online store for stylish and modest hijabs for all occasions. We blend fashion with faith.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Delivery times and rates vary depending on your location.

Can I return or exchange items?

Yes, returns and exchanges are accepted within 14 days of delivery. Please see our return policy for more details.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via our contact page or email us at support@hijabheaven.com.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website or through the courier's site.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards, COD and other secure payment methods depending on your region.

How do I choose the right hijab material?

Each product page includes fabric details and styling tips. You can also visit our Hijab Guide section to learn more about different materials like chiffon, cotton, jersey, and silk.

Do you offer discounts or promotions?

Yes! Subscribe to our newsletter to receive updates on sales, exclusive offers, and new arrivals. We also run seasonal promotions and offer discount codes from time to time.
